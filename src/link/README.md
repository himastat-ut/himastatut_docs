# LINK - Lintasan Inspirasi Nurturing Kegiatan

Welcome to the **LINK** program! This program is dedicated to bringing together students and preparing them for participation in statistics-related competitions. We focus on building teams, providing intensive training, and fostering collaboration among students. Below you will find the details and structure of the program.

## Program Overview

LINK (Lintasan Inspirasi Nurturing Kegiatan) is a program designed to inspire and nurture students in their journey to participate in various statistics competitions. By bringing students together and providing them with intensive training, this program aims to create strong teams that can represent HIMASTAT in academic and extracurricular competitions.

## Objectives
- Unite students with an interest in statistics competitions.
- Form teams to participate in local, national, and international competitions.
- Provide training and guidance to ensure students are well-prepared for competitions.
- Foster a sense of community and teamwork among participants.

## Program Details
- **Target Group**: Statistika students interested in competitions.
- **Team Formation**: Students are grouped based on their interest and skills to form effective competition teams.
- **Training Sessions**: Intensive training sessions to prepare students for competitions, covering relevant topics and problem-solving strategies.
- **Competitions**: Participation in a variety of statistics-related events, including but not limited to regional, national, and international competitions.

### Features of the Program
- **Collaboration**: Creating teams of students to collaborate and compete in statistics events.
- **Preparation**: Offering training and mentoring to participants, focusing on problem-solving and academic excellence.
- **Community Building**: Strengthening the bond between students through shared experiences and goals.
